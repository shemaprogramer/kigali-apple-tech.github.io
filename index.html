Our Works

Water Damage Repair

Accidents happen, and if your Apple device has encountered water damage, immediate action is crucial. Our technicians have the expertise to diagnose and repair water-damaged devices, saving your valuable data.

Battery Replacements

If your iPhone or MacBook is experiencing a battery that drains quickly or struggles to hold a charge, our experts can replace it with a genuine Apple battery, ensuring optimal performance.

Charging Port Repair

If your device is not charging or connecting properly to a power source, we can repair or replace the charging port, enabling you to power up your Apple device hassle-free.

Data Recovery

If you've accidentally deleted important files or your device has suffered from data loss, our skilled technicians can assist you in recovering your valuable data, ensuring minimal disruption to your work or personal life.

Mac Accessories Repair

From keyboards and trackpads to external drives and cables, we can repair various Mac accessories, allowing you to seamlessly integrate them into your Apple ecosystem.

Device Upgrade

Looking to boost the performance of your MacBook or iMac? We offer hardware upgrades, such as RAM and storage enhancements, to optimize your Apple device's capabilities

Ipad Repair Services

Whether your iPad has a cracked screen, battery problems, software glitches, or any other hardware-related issues, our skilled technicians are here to help. We use genuine Apple parts to ensure high-quality repairs and restore your iPad's functionality.

iWatch Repair Services

We provide reliable and precise iWatch repairs that exceed your expectations. Trust us to get your Apple Watch back in optimal working condition, so you can stay connected and make the most of your wearable technology.

Screen Replacement

Cracked or shattered screens are a common problem for Apple devices. We can swiftly replace your damaged screen, restoring your device's functionality and aesthetics.
